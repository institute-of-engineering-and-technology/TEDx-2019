About IET Lucknow

Established in 1984, Institute of Engineering and Technology, Lucknow is a State Government-funded engineering college in Lucknow. It is one of the premier engineering institutes of North India and is regarded as the top-ranked institute among all technical institutions affiliated/associated to AKTU Lucknow. Since the time of its incorporation of the first batch, the institute has been steadfastly involved in the field of education and has been imparting rich technical education and industrial training, to the engineering pursuants of state of Uttar Pradesh. To further a learner's technical aptitude, the institute also effects regular interactions with industry and academia. The college also boasts of a strong alumni base. The college was recognized as the best technical institute of North India in 2018 by Ministry of Human Resource and Development, Government of India.

TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four day conference in California in 1984, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. Today TED covers almost all disciplines and cultures across the globe. From science to business to global issues, TED is covered by over 100 languages, leaving footprint all over the world. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, Jack Ma, Stephen Hawking, Jane Goodall, JK Rolwing, George RR Martin, Kim Stanley, Hadi Edberk, Sal Khan, Sir Richard Branson, and many other visionaries. Two annual TED conferences invite the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less, of which most of the talks are made available at TED.com.

TEDx

x = independently organized TED event.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share TED like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These events are open source, local and community driven events that inspire innovation, through leadership and vidionsry insight.These local self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but the individual TEDx events are self-organized (Subject to Rules and Regulations).

TEDxIETLucknow

Helmed by an emergent group of curiously driven students of IET Lucknow, TEDxIETLucknow is a licensed, independently organized event. It will be, of many things, a platform for collection and dissemination of ideas worth spreading, across all domains; A symposium of established pioneers; A first intelligent fan to the flame of ideas in the city of Lucknow, the cultural capital of northern India.
